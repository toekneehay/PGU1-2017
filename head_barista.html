When striving to create the best cup of coffee on Earth, an important part of the process is the barista. We found are head barista buried in mountain of beans in the volcanic mountains of Brazil, hunting for the rarest of bean. Well that maybe fabricated a bit her name is Tina and she applied last year. We love Tina she strives to make a perfect coffee is open to new drinks and is a great teacher to our other baristas. Tina is like all of us, hoping to drink that perfect cup of coffee.
